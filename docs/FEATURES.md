## Patient Management Features

### 1. Patient Registration & Profiles
- **Add New Patient** - Register new patients with required information
- **Edit Patient Information** - Update existing patient details
- **Patient Directory** - Browse all registered patients
- **Patient Search** - Quick lookup using name, ID, or phone
- **Patient ID Generation** - Auto-generate unique patient identifiers
- **Duplicate Prevention** - System alerts for potential duplicate entries
- **Patient Status** - Track patient status (Active, Inactive, Archived)

### 2. Patient Information Storage
- **Personal Details** - Name, date of birth, gender, ID type/number
- **Contact Information** - Phone, email, address, emergency contacts
- **Insurance Information** - Insurance provider, policy number, coverage type
- **Medical History** - Previous conditions, surgeries, hospitalizations
- **Allergies & Sensitivities** - Document all drug and food allergies
- **Current Medications** - Track ongoing medication prescriptions
- **Immunization Records** - Vaccination history and due dates
- **Family History** - Hereditary conditions and family health background

### 3. Patient Communication
- **Appointment Reminders** - Automated SMS/email appointment notifications
- **Medical Alerts** - Important health notifications
- **Prescription Reminders** - Medication adherence reminders
- **Patient Portal** - Access to personal health information
- **Messaging System** - Doctor-patient secure messaging

### 4. Patient Access Control
- **View History** - See all past visits and interactions
- **Access Records** - Patient can view their health records
- **Export Data** - Download patient health information
- **Privacy Controls** - Manage who can access information
- **Consent Tracking** - Document patient consent for treatments
